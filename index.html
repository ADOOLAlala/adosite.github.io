About Ado

Early Life and Career Beginnings

Ado (born October 24, 2002) is a Japanese singer and songwriter who gained prominence with her distinctive voice and impactful music.
From a young age, Ado was captivated by music, particularly Vocaloid songs. In her early teens, she became interested in the culture of
"utaite"—singers who cover songs and share them online without revealing their identities. This fascination led her to start her own journey
as a singer.

On January 10, 2017, at the age of 14, Ado began her musical career by uploading a cover of the Vocaloid song "Kimi no Taion" to the Japanese
video-sharing platform Niconico. This marked the beginning of her presence in the online music community.

Breakthrough with "Usseewa"

Ado's major breakthrough came in 2020 when she released her debut single, "Usseewa," at the age of 17. The song quickly gained massive
popularity, resonating with a wide audience for its bold lyrics and powerful delivery. "Usseewa" topped several charts, including the Billboard
Japan Hot 100 and the Oricon Digital Singles Chart. It also reached 100 million plays on Billboard Japan within 17 weeks, making it one of
the fastest songs to achieve this milestone and establishing Ado as the youngest solo artist to do so.

Artistic Style and Persona

Ado is known for maintaining a sense of mystery by not revealing her face publicly, a practice common among "utaite" artists. Her music often
features a blend of rock, pop, and Vocaloid-inspired elements, characterized by her dynamic vocal performances and emotionally charged
delivery. She is particularly recognized for her powerful voice, which seamlessly transitions between aggressive, raw tones and softer, more
melodic expressions. Ado's songs frequently explore themes of rebellion, self-expression, and inner turmoil, resonating strongly with younger
audiences. Her theatrical style and ability to convey intense emotions have drawn comparisons to anime and stage performances, making her
a standout figure in the J-pop scene.

Recent Activities

As of February 2025, Ado continues to be an influential figure in the Japanese music scene. She has released multiple singles and albums,
collaborated with various artists, and her music videos have garnered significant attention online. Her commitment to her unique artistic
vision and her ability to connect with listeners have solidified her status as a prominent artist in contemporary Japanese music.

Beyond her studio work, Ado has expanded her presence on the global stage with multiple world tours. In 2023, she embarked on her first
world tour, Wish, performing in Japan and select international locations, marking her debut on the global live music scene. Following its
success, she announced her next world tour, Hibana—one of the largest world tours in history by a Japanese artist—set to take place in
Spring/Summer 2025. This tour will bring her performances to even more countries across North America, Europe, and Asia, further
cementing her status as an international artist. As with her previous tours, Hibana promises to deliver captivating live performances
while maintaining her signature air of mystery, using dynamic stage visuals and lighting effects to enhance the experience.
